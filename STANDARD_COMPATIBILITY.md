# Standard Compatibility

## VERIFRAX Does Not Define a Standard

VERIFRAX does **not** define a standard.

VERIFRAX outputs MAY be mapped by third parties.

Any "standard" interpretation is external.

## Third-Party Mappings

Third parties may:
- Map VERIFRAX outputs to other formats
- Interpret VERIFRAX outputs as compatible with standards
- Create mappings between VERIFRAX and other systems

## External Interpretation

All standard interpretations are external to VERIFRAX.

VERIFRAX does not:
- Claim standard status
- Claim compliance with standards
- Claim recognition by standards bodies
- Define standards

## Scope

VERIFRAX executes deterministic computation. Standard interpretation is external.
