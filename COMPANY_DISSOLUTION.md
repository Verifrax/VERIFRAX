# Company Dissolution

## Certificates Remain Valid

**Assertion:** Certificates remain valid after company dissolution.

**Proof:**
- Certificates are self-contained
- Verification is independent
- No corporate entity required
- Certificates remain valid

## Verification Claims Unchanged

**Assertion:** Verification claims unchanged after company dissolution.

**Proof:**
- Verification is deterministic
- Claims are cryptographic
- No corporate authority needed
- Claims remain unchanged

## No Reliance on Corporate Existence

**Assertion:** No reliance on corporate existence.

**Proof:**
- Certificates are independent
- Verification is independent
- No corporate services required
- No corporate authority needed

## Scope

Company dissolution affects:
- New certificate issuance (if company still exists)
- Support services (if company still exists)

Company dissolution does NOT affect:
- Existing certificates
- Certificate verification
- Evidence packages
- Verification claims

## Conclusion

Company dissolution has **zero impact** on existing certificates.

Certificates remain valid regardless of corporate status.
