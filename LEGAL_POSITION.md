# Legal Position

## What VERIFRAX Is

VERIFRAX is deterministic software that executes one-time verification runs on digital evidence bundles. The software produces cryptographically verifiable certificates.

## What VERIFRAX Is NOT

- Not legal advice
- Not financial advice
- Not auditing services
- Not escrow services
- Not prediction services
- Not advisory services

## Service Scope

VERIFRAX executes deterministic software algorithms. VERIFRAX does not:
- Provide advice
- Make judgments
- Hold custody of funds
- Guarantee outcomes
- Interpret results

## Execution Only

VERIFRAX performs technical execution only. Customer uploads evidence bundle. System executes verification algorithm. Output is certificate.

## No Warranties

VERIFRAX provides no warranties beyond execution of the deterministic algorithm. No guarantees of correctness, completeness, or fitness for purpose.

## Liability

Liability is limited to the execution fee paid. No other liability exists.

## Certificates

Certificates are customer-controlled artifacts. VERIFRAX does not control, modify, or interpret certificates after issuance.

## Plain Language Summary

VERIFRAX runs software. Customer pays. Software executes. Certificate is produced. No advice. No judgment. No guarantees.
