# VERIFRAX Outcome-Based Pricing

Pricing reflects eliminated uncertainty, not computation.

---

## Tier 1 — Verification Event

Price: EUR 120

Eliminated Uncertainty:
- Whether an evidence bundle satisfies a declared verification profile

Includes:
- Deterministic hashing
- Deterministic verdict
- Reproducible output

Excludes:
- Delivery acceptance
- Finality language
- Dispute resolution claims

---

## Tier 2 — Delivery Finality

Price: EUR 650

Eliminated Uncertainty:
- Whether delivery has been accepted

Includes:
- Verification Event
- VERIFRAX Delivery Certificate
- Finality statement
- Timestamped, portable proof

Effect:
- Dispute space collapses upon issuance

---

## Tier 3 — Institutional Delivery

Price: Custom

Eliminated Uncertainty:
- Cross-party disagreement
- Jurisdictional ambiguity
- Re-verification trust

Includes:
- Multiple evidence bundles
- Federation or independent re-verification
- Jurisdiction-aware documentation
- Audit-grade packaging
