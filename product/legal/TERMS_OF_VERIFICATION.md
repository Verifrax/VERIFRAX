# VERIFRAX Terms of Verification

VERIFRAX provides deterministic verification of submitted evidence bundles
against declared verification profiles.

VERIFRAX guarantees:
- Deterministic execution
- Reproducible results
- Cryptographic integrity of outputs

VERIFRAX does not guarantee:
- Correctness of input evidence
- Intent, authorship, or quality
- Legal compliance or regulatory approval

Verification results represent technical finality only.
