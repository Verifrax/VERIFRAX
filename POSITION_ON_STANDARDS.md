# Position on Standards

## Statement (Verbatim)

VERIFRAX does not publish standards.
VERIFRAX publishes executable software.
Interpretation and adoption are external.

## Scope

VERIFRAX:
- Executes deterministic computation
- Produces certificates
- Verifies computation

VERIFRAX does not:
- Define standards
- Claim standard status
- Seek standard recognition
- Assert compliance

## External Interpretation

Interpretation and adoption are external to VERIFRAX.

Third parties may:
- Interpret VERIFRAX outputs
- Map VERIFRAX to standards
- Adopt VERIFRAX outputs

All such activities are external.
