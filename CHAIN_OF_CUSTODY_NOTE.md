# Chain of Custody Note

## Principle (Verbatim)

VERIFRAX does not assert chain of custody.
VERIFRAX verifies computation only.
Custody assertions are external to VERIFRAX.

## Scope

VERIFRAX verifies:
- Hash computation
- Certificate generation
- Deterministic execution

VERIFRAX does not verify:
- Evidence authenticity
- Evidence custody
- Evidence chain of custody
- Evidence provenance

## External Assertions

Chain of custody assertions are external to VERIFRAX. VERIFRAX does not provide or verify such assertions.
